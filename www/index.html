Nightscout Clock

Turn your Ulanzi TC001 into a CGM display

What you need

  1. An Ulanzi TC001 pixel clock (buy here)
  2. A USB-C cable connected to this computer
  3. Google Chrome or Microsoft Edge browser

Install firmware

  1. Power on the Ulanzi TC001 by holding the < and > buttons for 3 seconds
  2. Connect it to this computer with a USB-C cable
  3. Click the Install button below and select the serial port

Ready? Plug in your clock and click Install.

Your browser does not support Web Serial.
Please open this page in Google Chrome or Microsoft Edge on a desktop computer.

After flashing

  1. The clock will restart and show a setup screen
  2. Connect to the NightscoutClock Wi-Fi network from your phone
  3. A setup page will open automatically (or go to 192.168.4.1)
  4. Enter your Wi-Fi credentials and Nightscout URL
  5. The clock will connect and start showing your glucose readings
Need your Nightscout URL? It looks like https://yoursite.herokuapp.com or https://yoursite.fly.dev
